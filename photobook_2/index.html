about me


My name is Tracy Fernandez.

I don’t remember a day in high school when I didn’t have my camera with me. I walked around with my Nikon Coolpix B400 without the slightest idea about how to take a good photograph. I didn’t care. I wanted to take as many photographs of my friends as I could fit into an 8 GB SD card, which I quickly realized wasn’t THAT many.

And, to this day, I still have that sentiment.
